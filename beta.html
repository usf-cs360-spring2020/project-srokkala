Question for Prototype 2

What are the most affordable places to live in the Bay Area for the average American according to statistics from the past 10 years

Encoding

To read this map, it is important to understand that the darker the red, the more expensive the cost of a house in the area is.

Answer

Bethel Island is the more affordable place to live in the Bay Area and has been for the past 10 years. We can see an upwards spike in house prices due to the rise of Silicon Valley.

Planned Interactivity

I would like to have a hoverable tooltip that would display the area and price of a house in the location for the past 10 years.
