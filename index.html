House Pricing in the United States

A Closer Look

It is the American Dream to be able to buy a house in the Unites States however as time passes this is becoming increasingly difficult. At some point in my life, I would like to buy a house in the United States however, different states have different price ranges. In addition to that, different cities in each state have different price ranges. Because of this inconsistency, I was inspired to use dataset that contained house prices of all states in order to show which states and cities have the most affordable for the average American who earn about 1.4 million dollars over the course of the careers.

By clicking any of the links in the navigation bar, you can see I have link to the dataset I've used, the prototype I've built, and a brief introduction about me
