Home Values

For my dataset, I've decided to use Zillow's Home Values dataset to accurately capture the housing price ranges. I've chosen Zillow because they're a trusted source when it comes to anything related to housing and they are the most used housing datasource. One can look up the address of any home in the United States and find the complete history of that house including construction date, sell prices, renovation dates and etc..

Here is the Link to my Dataset (Accessed on April 13th): Click Here

This dataset columns for Region Name, Region Rank, SizeRank, and monthly stats from 1996 April to 2020 February.

Some Data Wrangling I had to do was to get the average house prices for February 2020 (Latest Dataset).
